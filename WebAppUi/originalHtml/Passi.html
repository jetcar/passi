Welcome to Passi

Welcome to the future of secure and passwordless authentication with our OAuth2 service. Users begin by confirming their email address, after which our mobile app generates a certificate and securely sends its public part to our server. During authentication, our website verifies the user's signature on a random string, ensuring a seamless and secure login process. For an extra layer of security, users can approve the certificate thumbprint, transforming this into a zero-trust authentication system. Plus, our entire codebase is open-source and available on GitHub, allowing everyone to examine how it works for complete transparency and trust.
